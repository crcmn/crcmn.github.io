Our Community, Our Commitment

We are a grassroots organization committed to the health and wellbeing of our community. We serve children and families in the Southern Metro region of the Twin Cities and are part of the community we serve.

Request Support

EMPOWERING LIVES TOGETHER Community Resource Center

Our organization values culture, relationships, and the uniqueness of each person and family. We approach our work with humility, learning from the people we serve what is most important to them and connecting them to resources that support their health and wellbeing. From healthcare to employment opportunities, we link people to opportunities that help them thrive.

Join us in empowering people and communities by donating or volunteering to support our programs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and families by connecting them with essential resources that support holistic health and wellbeing.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every family has access to quality healthcare, family- sustaining employment, safe and affordable housing, and education. We are dedicated to removing barriers to access and supporting each familys success.

What We Do

Our programs are designed in response to community needs and focus on serving the whole family. Learn more about our programs and ways we can help.

Career Assistance

Support in finding and maintaining family-sustaining employment.

After-school PROGRAM

Enriching activities for children after school hours.

Food ACCESS

Resources and access to nutritious meals for individuals and families.

Housing ASSISTANCE

Support for individuals facing housing instability in finding and maintaining safe and affordable housing.
